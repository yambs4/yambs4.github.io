About Me

Professional background and expertise

I am an Economist (Ph.D.) with over 15 years of experience in data science, monitoring and evaluation (M&E), and research in development interventions. My work integrates rigorous methodologies with practical solutions to drive impactful outcomes. I have successfully led over 20 consultancy projects, managed large-scale surveys with 15K+ respondents, and published research on rural development and education in Ethiopia.
